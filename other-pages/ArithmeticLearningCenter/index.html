Addition is the "combined" or "total" amount of numbers.
Subtraction is the "difference" of two numbers. Or an easier way
of thinking about it is like this: Subtraction is what's left over
when you remove some amount of a number away from another amount of number.



Multiplication is the total when you add a number x, n times.
Division is the opposite of multiplication
